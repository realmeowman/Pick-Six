How Pick Six works

Each game picks one mystery player from the current sport. You have six guesses to find them using the clues you unlock along the way.

Make a guess and we’ll light up how close you are on league/conference, division, team, position, and the numeric stats – age, height, jersey number, or rings depending on the sport.

Clear four rounds in a row to go from stars to deep‑cut role players and golf nerd favorites.

Play with a friend: Click "Play with a friend", make a guess, then copy the link and send it (iMessage, text, etc.). Your friend opens it, makes their guess, and sends the link back. Take turns until you nail it — or run out of guesses.
