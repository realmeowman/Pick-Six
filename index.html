How to play

Each round is one mystery player from the sport you picked. You get six guesses; wrong guesses reveal clues so you can narrow it down.

After each guess we show how close you are on league or conference, division, team, position, and the numeric stats (age, height, number, rings, etc. — depends on the sport).

Rounds get harder as the player pool grows — from stars toward deeper cuts (and golf nerdery when you’re on Golf).

Co-op: Tap Co-op, make a guess, then copy the link and send it. Your friend opens it, guesses, and sends the link back — same round, back and forth until someone nails it or you run out of guesses.

Vs Mode: Share a link so a friend plays the same six rounds (same answers each round). Compare totals when you are both done — or challenge them to beat your final score from the link.

Share: Sends a link to this sport so anyone can play their own game.
